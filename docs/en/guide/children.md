* [Bilingual nursery at the "Dar" Centre](http://darcentre.co.uk/). Located on Abbey Road near the famous crossing in a church building nearby. You can enroll even toddlers. A very good nursery with attentive staff and many developmental games and activities. Almost everything is in Russian, but there are English lessons. * [Bilingual school "Azbuka"](http://www.azbukafoundation.org/). Located near Ravenscourt Park Underground station (less than 100 meters). Has a nursery, reception class (for 4-year-olds), and classes from Year 1 to Year 6 (ages 5-11). Teaching is in both Russian and English. The curriculum is British, so there won't be problems continuing education. Part of the program is Russian (mathematics, Russian language, and some other elements).

There are Saturday schools for children who attend English schools.
